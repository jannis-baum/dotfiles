# My keyboard layers

## Special keys

```plain
+--------------+----------+--------+-------------+
| cap          | held     | single | macbook key |
+--------------+----------+--------+-------------+
| native vi    | ctrl+opt | escape | capslock    |
+--------------+----------+--------+-------------+
| karabiner vi | ctrl     | ctrl+s | capslock    |
+--------------+----------+--------+-------------+

      +---------------+--------+-------------+
      | held          | single | macbook key |
+-----+---------------+--------+-------------+
| spc | caps layer    | space  | spacebar    |
+-----+---------------+--------+-------------+
| spn | numchar layer | space  | right cmd   |
+-----+---------------+--------+-------------+
```

## Base

```plain
+---+---+---+---+---+---+             +---+---+---+---+---+---+ 
|tab| q | w | e | r | t |             | y | u | i | o | p |del| 
+---+---+---+---+---+---+             +---+---+---+---+---+---+ 
|cap| a | s | d | f | g |             | h | j | k | l | ; | ' | 
+---+---+---+---+---+---+             +---+---+---+---+---+---+ 
|kit| z | x | c | v | b |             | n | m | , | . | / |ret| 
+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+     +---+---+---+---+---+---+---+ 
                |opt|spc|cap|     |cap|spn|opt|
                +---+---+---+     +---+---+---+
```
